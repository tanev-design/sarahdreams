# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Adults (primarily men) looking for a discreet, high-end sensual companionship or massage experience in Switzerland. Most will visit on mobile, often in a hurry or in private moments. They want to quickly confirm who Sarah is, what she offers, how much it costs, and how to book — without confusion, registration, or visual clutter.

## Product Purpose

A single-page brand site that builds immediate trust, presents services and pricing clearly, and makes booking as frictionless as possible. Success means a visitor can book an appointment or make a call within seconds of landing.

## Brand Personality

Warm, discreet, sophisticated, confident, human.

Voice is intimate but never crude; elegant but not cold; inviting but not desperate. The site should feel like a private invitation, not an advertisement.

## Anti-references

- Dark neon / red-light brothel aesthetics
- Cluttered classified-ad listings with dense text
- Loud autoplay animations or bouncing CTAs
- Cheap all-caps discount flyers
- Overly clinical medical-spa sterility
- Endless paragraphs of explicit detail before the offer is clear

## Design Principles

1. **Discretion through elegance** — tasteful imagery, no explicit hero shots, gallery feels like a private portfolio.
2. **One-tap conversion** — phone and booking buttons are visible in the header, hero, and sticky mobile bar.
3. **Human warmth** — soft blush/cream palette, serif display + sans body, personal first-person copy.
4. **Mobile-first clarity** — thumb-friendly targets, scannable cards, no horizontal scroll.
5. **Service hierarchy at a glance** — prices and durations are scannable within two seconds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast for all body text and interactive elements
- Keyboard-navigable menu, gallery lightbox, and form controls
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Meaningful alt text on every image
- Touch targets ≥ 44×44 px on mobile
